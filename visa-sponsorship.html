UK Visa Pathways

Two Routes. One Goal: Stay & Work in the UK

🎓

Graduate Visa

Post-Study Work Route

You've just completed your UK degree. The graduate visa gives you up to 2 years to find sponsored employment.

  • Duration: Up to 2 years post-graduation
  • No sponsor licence required yet
  • Time to find employer willing to sponsor
  • Transition to Skilled Worker thereafter
💼

Skilled Worker Visa

UK Employer Sponsorship

Once you have a job offer from a UK employer with a sponsor licence, you can apply for this visa to work long-term.

  • Employer must hold active sponsor licence
  • Role must meet skill and salary requirements
  • Multi-year work authorisation
  • Pathway to settlement and citizenship
