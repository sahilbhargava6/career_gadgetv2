About The Career Gadget

Bridge the gap between talent and opportunity.

The Career Gadget is a UK-based, end-to-end career services company founded in 2024. We work entirely on behalf of the candidate, providing the structured, expert-led support that should exist but doesn't — turning the chaos of job searching into a clear, managed, results-driven process.

Our Story

Founded in 2024 to Solve a Real Problem

UK universities do not provide job placements. Every year, thousands of graduates enter one of the world's most competitive job markets with no structured support, no guidance, and no idea where to start. The Career Gadget was built to solve this problem. Unlike traditional recruitment agencies that work on behalf of employers, we work entirely on behalf of the candidate — in your corner from day one. We combine hiring-side expertise with modern career strategy to help you land the role you deserve.
